FAVICON SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
==========================

Your chrome J logo is ready! Here's how to add it:

STEP 1: Create the favicon sizes
---------------------------------
You need to resize your image to these specific sizes:

1. favicon-16x16.png (16x16 pixels) - browser tab
2. favicon-32x32.png (32x32 pixels) - browser tab retina
3. apple-touch-icon.png (180x180 pixels) - iOS home screen

STEP 2: How to resize
---------------------
Option A - Use an online tool:
- Go to https://realfavicongenerator.net/
- Upload your chrome J image
- Download the generated files

Option B - Use Photoshop/Figma:
- Open your image
- Image > Image Size
- Change to each size above
- Export as PNG with transparent background (or black background to match your site)
- Save with the exact filenames above

STEP 3: Upload to your site
---------------------------
Put these 3 files in your public/ folder:
- public/favicon-16x16.png
- public/favicon-32x32.png
- public/apple-touch-icon.png

STEP 4: Deploy
--------------
Push to GitHub and Vercel will automatically serve them!
